Welcome to Jagdamba Group

Building Tomorrow's Infrastructure with Excellence

Trusted by Industry Leaders

Jagdamba Group is a premier engineering and construction firm established in 2017, specializing in high-quality civil engineering projects across Rajasthan. Delivering a working capacity of 2,195 MW through completed projects., we've built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and superior craftsmanship.

Our expertise encompasses critical infrastructure development including solar power installations, excavation, dams, canals, buildings, and roads. As trusted partners to industry giants like Tata Power, Adani Green Energy, Amp Energy, JK Cement, Eden Renewables, and Hartek Power, we consistently deliver projects that exceed expectations.
