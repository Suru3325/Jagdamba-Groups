Our Story

Building excellence in construction since 2017

A Vision of Excellence

Jagdamba Group was established in 2017 in Jodhpur, Rajasthan, with a vision to become a leading name in the civil engineering and construction industry. Under the visionary leadership of our founder and director, Mr. Bheru Singh Bhati, we began our journey with a small team and big dreams.

Starting with smaller projects, we quickly built a reputation for quality, reliability, and timely delivery. This foundation helped us grow steadily, expanding our capabilities and taking on increasingly complex projects across various sectors.

Today, we stand proud as a trusted name in construction, Having successfully delivered renewable energy projects totaling more than 3,070 MW of installed capacity. Our expertise in solar infrastructure construction has made us a preferred partner for major renewable energy companies like Tata Power Solar, Adani Green Energy, and Amp Energy India.

Our growth story is built on our commitment to excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction. We continue to invest in modern equipment, skilled personnel, and advanced methodologies to deliver exceptional construction solutions.

Mission & Vision

Our guiding principles that drive our every action

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, high-quality construction solutions that exceed client expectations through skilled workmanship, technical excellence, and unwavering commitment to safety and sustainability. We aim to create lasting value for our clients while fostering growth for our employees and communities where we operate.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Rajasthan's premier construction company known for excellence, reliability, and innovation. We aspire to expand our presence across India, setting industry benchmarks in construction quality and client satisfaction while pioneering sustainable construction practices across all our projects.

Our Leadership

The visionary behind our success

Bheru Singh Bhati

Founder & Director

Bheru Singh Bhati is the visionary founder and director of Jagdamba Group. With his strong entrepreneurial mindset and deep involvement in project operations, he established the company in 2017 with a mission to deliver high-quality construction services across Rajasthan.

Drawing from his business acumen and practical experience in handling on-ground operations, Mr. Bhati has been instrumental in guiding the company's growth trajectory. His hands-on leadership style and commitment to excellence have been key factors in Jagdamba Group's rapid ascent in the construction sector.

Under his leadership, the company has successfully executed renewable energy projects with a total installed capacity of over 3,070 MW, growing from a small firm to a respected name in the construction industry, particularly in the renewable energy sector. His vision continues to drive innovation and quality across all company operations.

Mr. Bhati’s focus on building lasting client relationships, adopting modern practices, and developing a skilled workforce has established Jagdamba Group as a trusted partner for complex construction projects across Rajasthan.
