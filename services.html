Civil Works

Piling Works

Specialized piling solutions to create strong and stable foundations for heavy structures. Our expertise ensures reliable support for solar MMS structures, transmission towers, industrial buildings, and other infrastructure projects, even in challenging soil conditions.

Industrial Construction

We specialize in industrial construction with a focus on quality and precision. Our team handles everything from planning to execution of complex industrial structures like control rooms, SCADA rooms, and more. We adhere to strict quality standards and project timelines to ensure client satisfaction.

Building Construction

Our building construction services cover commercial, industrial, and infrastructure projects. We handle foundation work, structural construction, finishing, and interior development. Each project is managed by experienced professionals to ensure quality, safety, and timely completion.

Boundary Walls

We construct durable and secure boundary walls for industrial complexes, solar parks, and commercial properties. Our boundary wall solutions include RR masonry walls and chain link fencing, designed to provide security while complementing the overall project aesthetics.

Excavation & Foundations

Excavation in All Strata

We provide comprehensive excavation services in all types of terrain and soil conditions. Our team is equipped with advanced machinery to handle challenging excavation projects including mass excavation, trench excavation, and precision digging for foundations and underground structures.

Hard Rock Excavation & Cutting

Our specialized rock cutting services include controlled blasting and mechanical cutting methods for efficient hard rock removal. We ensure minimal disturbance to surrounding areas while achieving precise excavation results for foundations, roads, and other construction needs.

Area Grading and Filling

We provide precise area grading and filling services to prepare construction sites for development. Our team uses advanced surveying tools and equipment to achieve the exact elevations and slopes required for proper drainage and stable foundations.

Solar Infrastructure

Solar PV Module Structure Foundations

We specialize in designing and constructing foundations for solar PV module structures. Our engineering team ensures these foundations are built to withstand environmental conditions while providing optimal support for solar arrays, taking into account soil conditions, wind loads, and other critical factors.

Control Room & SCADA Room Construction

We build specialized control rooms and SCADA rooms for solar power plants with precision and attention to detail. These facilities are designed to house sensitive electronic equipment in a controlled environment, with proper cooling, power backup systems, and security features.

Battery Room Construction

Our battery room construction services for solar projects focus on safety and efficiency. We design and build specialized rooms with proper ventilation, temperature control, acid-resistant flooring, and safety features to ensure optimal battery performance and longevity.

Water Projects

Dam & Canal Work

We have extensive experience in constructing dams and canals of various sizes. Our team handles everything from small irrigation dams to larger water management structures, ensuring structural integrity, safety, and environmental compliance throughout the project lifecycle.

Rainwater Harvesting Tanks

We design and construct effective rainwater harvesting systems including collection tanks, filtration systems, and distribution networks. Our solutions are customized to maximize water collection and storage while maintaining water quality for various applications.

Water Tanks & Septic Systems

We build durable water storage tanks, septic tanks, and soak pits for industrial and commercial properties. Our water management solutions ensure clean water storage and proper waste management with adherence to environmental regulations and health standards.

Infrastructure

Road Construction (WBM)

We specialize in Water Bound Macadam (WBM) road construction for industrial sites, solar parks, and commercial properties. Our road construction services include proper base preparation, compaction, and finishing to ensure durable, all-weather access roads that withstand heavy traffic and environmental conditions.

Stone Culverts & Drains

We construct stone culverts and drainage systems designed to manage water flow effectively across roads and properties. Our drainage solutions ensure proper water management, prevent erosion, and extend the lifespan of roads and surrounding infrastructure.

HT/LT/LV Cable Trenches

We provide specialized cable trenching services for high, low, and medium voltage electrical systems. Our cable trenches are designed with proper dimensions, reinforcement, and drainage to protect critical power infrastructure while allowing for future maintenance and expansion.

Electrical Foundations

Transmission Line Works

We provide complete transmission line solutions, covering detailed route surveys, tower foundations, erection, stringing, and final testing. With a focus on precision and quality, our TL works ensure safe and efficient power evacuation, supporting large-scale solar and infrastructure projects with reliable connectivity to the grid.

Transformer Foundations

We design and construct robust foundations for various transformer types and sizes. Our transformer foundations are built to exact specifications, considering load requirements, soil conditions, and safety standards to ensure stable and secure transformer installations.

Street Light Poles & Monitoring Structures

We install foundations for street lighting, weather monitoring systems, and pyranometers used in solar projects. These specialized foundations are designed to provide stability and protection for sensitive equipment while ensuring proper alignment and orientation.

Panel Foundations & HTRMU

We construct foundations for electrical panels, High Tension Ring Main Units (HTRMU), and line isolators. These foundations are built with precision to accommodate electrical equipment while providing proper access for maintenance and operation.

Security

Security Services

We offer complete security management for solar plants, industrial facilities, and offices. Our services include deployment of trained security guards, 24/7 site monitoring, and strict access control to prevent unauthorized entry. We ensure safe movement of materials and equipment, protect valuable assets, and maintain a secure working environment for staff and contractors. With customized security planning as per project scale, we provide reliable protection for smooth and uninterrupted operations.

Support Services

Land Leasing

We facilitate land leasing services by coordinating with local landowners and communities. Our team ensures smooth agreements and reliable land availability, enabling companies to set up projects without delays or complications.

Land & DGPS Surveys

We offer comprehensive land surveying services using Differential Global Positioning System (DGPS) and Total Station equipment. Our survey teams provide accurate site measurements, topographical data, and boundary demarcation to support project planning and design activities.

Module Mounting & Installation

Through our associate firms, we provide module mounting and installation services for solar projects. This includes precise positioning, secure mounting, and electrical integration of solar panels to maximize energy production and system longevity.

Tours & Travel Services

We provide organized tour and tourism solutions, offering safe and comfortable travel experiences. Our services include guided tours, customized travel plans, and complete hospitality support to ensure hassle-free journeys. From corporate trips to leisure travel, we deliver professional arrangements for memorable experiences.
